UPI fraud is one of the most common cybercrimes in India. In this type of fraud, scammers trick people into sending money using UPI apps.

Typically, the fraudster contacts the victim by call or message pretending to be a bank official, customer, or buyer. They send a payment request and convince the victim that they need to approve it to receive money. In reality, approving the request deducts money from the victim's account.

Students and online sellers are common targets because they frequently use digital payments. Many people fall for this scam due to lack of awareness or urgency created by the fraudster.

The consequences include financial loss, stress, and sometimes loss of trust in digital platforms. Such frauds are increasing rapidly, making it important for everyone to stay alert and informed.
